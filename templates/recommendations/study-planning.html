Study Planning

Effective strategies to manage academic workload and reduce stress

A well-structured study plan can significantly reduce academic stress by breaking tasks into manageable segments and promoting consistent progress.

Key Strategies
  • Pomodoro Technique: Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After four cycles, take a longer break.
  • Chunking: Break large assignments into smaller, actionable tasks.
  • Prioritization: Use to-do lists and prioritize by deadline and importance.
  • Time Blocking: Allocate specific time slots for studying, rest, and other activities.
Tips
  • Set realistic goals for each study session
  • Avoid cramming; spread learning over time
  • Create a dedicated study space free from distractions
Helpful Resources
  • Pomofocus – Free Pomodoro timer
  • Todoist – Task management app
  • Toggl – Time tracking for productivity
