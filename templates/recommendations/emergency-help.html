Emergency Help & Crisis Resources

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out immediately

Important: This system does not provide emergency services. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline.
Crisis Helplines (24/7)
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (US)
    1-800-273-8255 • 988lifeline.org
  • Crisis Text Line
    Text HOME to 741741 • crisistextline.org
  • International Association for Suicide Prevention
    Find a crisis center near you
When to Seek Immediate Help
  • Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
  • Severe anxiety or panic that feels unmanageable
  • Inability to perform daily activities
  • Withdrawal from social contact for an extended period
Campus Resources

Many universities offer 24/7 crisis support. Check your institution's health services or counseling center website for local emergency contacts.
