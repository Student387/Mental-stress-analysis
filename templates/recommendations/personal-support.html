Personal Support

Building connections and seeking professional support when needed

Social support and professional counseling are vital for managing stress. Connecting with others and accessing mental health services can make a significant difference.

Building Social Support
  • Join campus clubs, study groups, or online communities
  • Schedule regular calls or meetups with family and friends
  • Reach out to peers; many students face similar challenges
Professional Support

University counseling centers often offer free or low-cost sessions. Seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness.

  • Visit your campus health or counseling center
  • Consider teletherapy if in-person sessions are not available
  • Speak with a trusted advisor, professor, or mentor
Helpful Resources
  • BetterHelp – Online counseling platform
  • 7 Cups – Free emotional support and counseling
