Meditation & Mindfulness

Evidence-based practices for stress reduction and mental clarity

Meditation and mindfulness techniques have been scientifically shown to reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance overall well-being among students.

Benefits
  • Reduces anxiety and stress hormones
  • Improves concentration and academic performance
  • Enhances emotional regulation
  • Promotes better sleep quality
Getting Started

Begin with 5–10 minutes daily. Find a quiet space, sit comfortably, and focus on your breath. When your mind wanders, gently redirect attention back to your breathing.

Helpful Resources
  • Headspace – Guided meditation app with student discounts
  • Calm – Sleep stories, meditation, and breathing exercises
  • Mindful.org – Free mindfulness guides
